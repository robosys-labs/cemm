# Review of the Existing `3.3-uol-graph-architecture.md`

**Baseline reviewed:** `robosys-labs/cemm@c6675ee5e822c4af6253e1bce2e76975fed0f9c9`  
**Conclusion:** retain the original foundation, replace the document.

## What remains correct

The existing document correctly establishes that:

- `UOLGraph` is temporary working memory;
- fixed atom kinds are representation categories, not domain concepts;
- edge vocabulary should remain small;
- concepts, predicates, ports, constructions, and affordances are durable compressed machinery;
- predicted effects are not static ports;
- durable mutation must pass through `GraphPatch`;
- the graph should preserve ambiguity and multiple meaning groups.

These principles are preserved in the replacement.

## Why a substantial update is required

The existing document is aligned to package-level v3.1 architecture and ends the graph pipeline with `ActResolutionPlanner`, graph-patch extraction, and asynchronous consolidation. That conflicts with the latest 3.3 design.

The primary corrections are:

1. **Add authority states.** Graph presence must not imply grounded or executable meaning.
2. **Replace atom-only candidate selection with interpretation branches.** Ambiguity commonly concerns subgraphs, scope, grammar, role orientation, and ports.
3. **Add proposition-level scope.** Source, permission, time, modality, polarity, quotation, and completion must bind to a proposition, not merely exist somewhere in the graph.
4. **Separate candidate action atoms from predicate/operator activation.**
5. **Move state occupancy/transmutation authority outside raw graph atoms.**
6. **Add typed semantic gaps and recursive learning hooks.**
7. **Treat articles, prepositions, morphology, and other grammar as semantic graph operators.**
8. **Move graph-patch compilation after obligation and contract authorization.**
9. **Make concept resolution, entity grounding, and port bindings branch-scoped.**
10. **Replace one planner with operational meaning, causal effects, obligation graph, and single contract compilation.**
11. **Expand training export to include rejected branches, gaps, state, contracts, use outcomes, and patch results.**
12. **Correct the invalid `edge_type = relation` example.**
13. **Replace current-world political examples with stable structural examples.**
14. **Add performance design for shared graph plus immutable branch deltas.**

## Severity

- P0: graph-builder patch emission; direct action/state consequence; placeholder port authority; missing proposition scope.
- P1: no branch-scoped grounding; no semantic-gap/acquisition model; single function/selected-atom collapse.
- P2: stale planner terminology, training-export gaps, examples, status header, and index list.
